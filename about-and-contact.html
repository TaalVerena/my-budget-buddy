How It Works

  1. Get Started:
    Click on the 'Let's Go' button on the home page. A pop-up will appear where you can enter your income and budget.
  2. Pay Period & Currency:
    Select your pay period and currency from the drop-down and see your preferences update throughout My Budget Buddy.
  3. Income & Budget:
    Fill in your income and allocate your budget for different categories like Rent, Transport, Food, Utilities, and others.
  4. Review and Adjust:
    Once your income and budget details are in, My Budget Buddy does a quick calculation and if your budget exceeds your income, we will alert you, helping ensure you're setting achievable budget goals. Click the 'Next' button to move on to entering your expenses.
  5. Expenses:
    Enter your expenses for each category and click the 'Next' button to see your results.
  6. Results:
    • Summary:
      See your results in a clear and concise format with a summary of your total income, budget, spending and savings.
    • Stay On Track:
      My Budget Buddy keeps you on track by showing the comparison of your spending versus your budget. It will highlight areas where you're under or over your budget, allowing you to make informed decisions for your future spending.
    • Visualize Your Spending:
      A handy graph shows your spending in relation to your income for each category. This clear and concise information helps you easily see where your money is going, allowing for effortless adjustments to your spending habits.

My Budget Buddy is not just a budget tracker, it's a companion in your financial journey, helping you achieve your financial goals while keeping the process relaxed and straightforward. Try it today and take a step towards financial freedom and peace of mind!
